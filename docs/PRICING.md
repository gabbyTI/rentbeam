# RentBeam Pricing

## Pricing Model

RentBeam operates on a **per-transaction fee model** with no monthly subscriptions, setup fees, or hidden costs.

---

## Fee Structure

### Landlords Pay Nothing Upfront
- ✅ **$0** monthly subscription
- ✅ **$0** setup fees
- ✅ **$0** per unit/property
- ✅ **$0** for unlimited tenants
- ✅ **$0** for payment tracking and reporting

### Pay Only When You Get Paid

**Online Card Payments (Autopay or One-Time)**
- **2.9% + $0.30** per transaction
- Covers Stripe processing fees + RentBeam platform fee
- Deducted automatically from each payment
- Example: $1,500 rent = $43.80 fee → landlord receives $1,456.20

**Manual Payments (Cash, Check, Interac e-Transfer)**
- **100% FREE** - No fees for marking manual payments as received
- Use RentBeam to track these payments at no cost

---

## Who Pays the Fee?

### Standard Model (Landlord Absorbs Fee)
The **landlord receives** rent amount minus the 2.9% + $0.30 fee.

**Example:**
- Tenant pays: $1,500
- Processing fee: $43.80
- Landlord receives: $1,456.20

### Pass-Through Option (Coming Soon)
Landlords can choose to pass the fee to tenants:
- Tenant pays: $1,543.80 ($1,500 + $43.80 fee)
- Landlord receives: $1,500 (full rent amount)

*Note: Check local regulations - some jurisdictions prohibit passing credit card fees to tenants.*

## Fair Pricing Philosophy

**Why per-transaction pricing?**
1. **Pay Only for Value** - You're charged when tenants pay online (the most convenient method)
2. **Free Manual Tracking** - No penalty for receiving cash/check/Interac
3. **No Barrier to Entry** - Start with zero risk and zero upfront cost
4. **Scale Friendly** - Fees don't increase just because you add more properties
5. **Transparent** - One simple rate, no complicated tier structure

**Our goal:** Make professional rent collection accessible to landlords of all sizes without the overhead of traditional property management.

## Billing & Invoicing

### How Fees Are Collected
1. Tenant pays rent via card (e.g., $1,500)
2. Stripe processes payment and deducts fee ($43.80)
3. Net amount deposited to your Stripe balance ($1,456.20)
4. Funds auto-transfer to your bank (2 business days)

### Monthly Statement
- View all transactions in your Stripe dashboard
- Export to CSV for accounting
- Filter by tenant, property, date range
- Track fees paid vs. net revenue

## Questions?

**Q: Can I negotiate the rate for high volume?**
A: Contact us at support@rentbeam.ca for enterprise pricing (50+ units).

**Q: What if a payment fails?**
A: No fee charged on failed payments. Autopay retries automatically (no extra fee).

**Q: Can I get a refund on fees if I refund a tenant?**
A: Stripe's 2.9% + $0.30 fee is non-refundable (industry standard), but we don't add additional charges.

**Q: Do you take a cut of late fees?**

A: The entire platform is free until you collect your first online payment. Use it risk-free!
